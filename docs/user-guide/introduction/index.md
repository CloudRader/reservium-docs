### What is the difference between Users and Managers?

Reservium distinguishes between two roles to ensure proper access control:

!!! info "User vs. Manager"
    - **Users**: Can view calendars, create reservations, and manage their own events.
    - **Managers**: Have extended capabilities, such as access to the **Manager Panel**, approving/declining reservations, and configuring service rules.

    Detailed manager functionality is covered separately in the [Manager Guide](../../manager-guide/introduction/index.md).
